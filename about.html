About Us

Welcome to **FoodBridge**, a platform designed to bring people together to fight food waste and hunger. Every day, countless restaurants, businesses, and individuals have surplus food that goes uneaten while millions struggle to find their next meal. At FoodBridge, we aim to connect these dots by providing a convenient platform for donating and accessing food.

Our Mission: To create a world where no food goes to waste, and no one goes hungry. FoodBridge serves as a bridge between those with surplus food and those who need it most, including the poor, elderly, and orphanages.

How It Works: Restaurants, individuals, and organizations can upload details of their surplus food onto our platform. Those in need, or caretakers of vulnerable communities, can browse these listings and claim food in real-time. FoodBridge ensures a seamless process to make sure the food reaches its destination quickly and safely.
